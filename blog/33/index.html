Foxkeh's Blog is here!

2007/05/01 Comments (0) Trackbacks (0)

Hello everyone! Have you heard of Foxkeh? Foxkeh was born to boost the promotion of Firefox in Japan and has quite popular on the Internet in Japan.

Last September Foxkeh was born and we and collected ideas for his name from over 7,000 community members. He was named Foxkeh after FIREFOX and SUKE, the suffix of common boy's name in Japan (e.g. Daisuke Matsuzaka.) Since then, he has appeared in the VOX Banner Contest, a time chart document, an nonfiction novel on the history of Firefox, monthly wallpapers, a series of promotional videos, and much more.

In response to public demand, we have decided to localize this Japanese content into English. The time chart of Firefox history and Foxkeh's Wallpapers are avaliable today. English-dubbed version of our video series will be broadcast here. Be sure to check it out!

More videos and Firefox affiliate banners featuring Foxkeh are coming soon. Subscribe our web feed in your favorite feed reader to keep up with the latest information. Thanks for visiting Foxkeh's blog and thank you for promoting Firefox.

- The Mozilla Japan Team
